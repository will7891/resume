About Colleen Quisumbing

I have a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from San Jose State University, and a Master's Degree in Counseling from CSU East Bay. I began providing counseling services in 2008 and became a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist in 2012. I have experience providing therapy to adults and children who have faced a variety of challenges in life and in their relationships. I currently provide psychotherapy services at my private practice location in downtown Sunnyvale, and am also a Clinical Director at the Center for Learning and Autism Support Services.
